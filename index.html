My Story

Born and raised in the heart of Milan, Alessandro Rossi blends traditional Italian tailoring with avant-garde structuralism. His journey began in the historic ateliers of Via Montenapoleone, where he mastered the silent language of fabric and form. . His work is an invitation to experience fashion as a living art—effortless, sophisticated, and eternally modern. Alessandro also shares his creations and inspirations regularly on his social media, giving a window into his creative world.
